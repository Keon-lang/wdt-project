About Us

About Us

More than 15 years ago, we had a vision. We wanted to be the pioneer of the online learning platform in Malaysia. Hence, here we are now, the pioneer in online learning that covers almost all subjects in the Malaysian syllabus. Throughout the years, our company has worked closely together with many states bringing education closer to students via the provision of back-end LMS engine for hosting online learning content for Primary 1 – 6 and Form 1 – 5, powered by MyET LMS Digital Education Solutions.

Our Mission

We aim to provide online learning content to cater to the whole of our country. Collaborating closely with MOE, it is our goal to enable all Malaysian students to access our portal, at ANYTIME and ANYWHERE, be it in the cities or rural areas of Malaysia. In the fast-growing technology phase, the internet will no longer be a jargon word even for rural students. Connecting them to online learning and allowing them to enjoy its great advantages is a great mission for our team.
